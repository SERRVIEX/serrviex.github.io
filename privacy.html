Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications ("Apps"), including games, utilities, family safety apps, and other software available on the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and other platforms.

We offer different types of Apps:

  • General Apps: Games, utilities, and other applications designed for users aged 13 and older
  • Family Safety Apps: Applications designed for families to share locations and stay connected. Families may consist entirely of adults, or may include children with appropriate safeguards

Some sections of this Privacy Policy apply specifically to one type of App, as noted. By downloading, installing, or using our Apps, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Apps.

Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously. For detailed information about how we protect children in family-oriented Apps, please see our Children's Safety Policy.

General Apps

Our general-purpose Apps (games, utilities) are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in these Apps. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

Family Safety Apps

Some of our Apps are family safety applications that enable families to share locations and stay connected. These Apps support families of all compositions - including families consisting entirely of adults, as well as families that include children. For families with only adult members (18+), standard privacy practices apply with no age-based restrictions.

When children participate in family groups, additional safeguards apply as described below.

Information We Collect When Children Participate

All users, including children, create their own accounts. During registration, users provide their age or birthdate for age verification purposes. When a child (under 13) creates an account and joins a family group (with parental consent), we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name or nickname, email address (for login), age/birthdate (for verification), profile picture (optional)
  • Authentication: Children may sign in using email, Sign in with Google, or Sign in with Apple
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location to enable family location sharing features
  • User-Generated Content: Chat messages sent within family groups (children under 13 have limited content creation abilities)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version (for functionality and troubleshooting)
  • Usage Data: App usage information to provide and improve the service

This information is collected solely to provide the family safety features and is not used for advertising purposes. Children can chat with family members and view content created by other family members. Children under 13 have access to limited app functionality and certain features require parental verification to use. All interactions are limited to their family group - they cannot interact with anyone outside the group.

How We Protect Children's Data

The following protections apply when a family includes children under 13:

  • Age Verification: All users provide their age or birthdate during account creation to verify eligibility
  • Adult-Created Groups: Only adults (18+) can create family groups
  • Parental Consent: Parents must verify their email and actively consent before a child under 13 can participate
  • Limited Functionality: Children under 13 have access to limited app features and certain actions require parental verification
  • Parent-Controlled: Parents maintain full control over their children's data and can view, modify, or delete it at any time
  • Purpose-Limited: Children's data is used only for family safety features, never for advertising targeting or profiling
  • No Advertising: Users identified as children under 13 do not see any advertisements in our Apps
  • No Advertising ID Collection: We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID) from users identified as children under 13
  • Limited Retention: Location history is retained for up to 10 days; other data is deleted when the user deletes their account
  • Parental Rights: Parents can review, download, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's data at any time

COPPA Compliance

For Apps that may be used by children, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and similar regulations in other jurisdictions. This includes:

  • Providing notice to parents about our data practices before collecting information from children
  • Requiring parental consent (via email verification and active confirmation) before a child under 13 can participate
  • Giving parents the right to review, delete, and refuse further collection
  • Maintaining confidentiality and security of children's information
  • Retaining children's data only as long as necessary
